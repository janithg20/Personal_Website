01 / About

Built for scale. Tested for trust.

Janith Gunasekara is a Quality Engineering and Technology Leader with 20+ years of experience helping organizations deliver high-quality software at scale. Across telecommunications, healthcare, e-commerce, retail, logistics, food services and financial technology, he's led enterprise-wide quality transformation initiatives that turned traditional QA functions into modern engineering disciplines.

As Head of Quality & Release Engineering at GTN Technologies, Janith drives quality strategy, release governance, automation, performance engineering, DevOps practices and engineering excellence across global technology teams — embedding quality throughout the SDLC while enabling rapid, reliable delivery.

At Circles.Life, Janith drove enterprise quality transformation across a global telco-tech platform — building automation and performance engineering capability behind multiple MVNO launches across markets. At Sysco LABS, he built and scaled quality engineering practices supporting large-scale, multi-billion-dollar supply chain and logistics platforms. Earlier leadership roles at Velou Inc and Cambio Healthcare Systems saw him build high-performing engineering organizations and introduce automation and continuous delivery at enterprise scale — leading global teams of 500+ engineers along the way.

He's also an active thought leader, conference speaker, and mentor, sharing insights on Quality Engineering, DevOps, AI in software engineering, and engineering leadership.
